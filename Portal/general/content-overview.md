# Content Workspace

All content production for the PromptEngines ecosystem.

```
content/
├── promptengines/        ← Content for promptengines.com
│   ├── experiments/      ← Write-ups of AI experiments and findings
│   ├── updates/          ← Major project/product update posts
│   └── projects/         ← Project spotlights and case studies
└── lab/                  ← lab.promptengines.com articles
    ├── queue/            ← Source material / articles sent in to be written up
    ├── drafts/           ← Articles in progress
    └── published/        ← Finished, published articles (for record)
```

---

## Workflow

### promptengines.com Content
1. Andy watches the GitHub repo for new commits
2. Identifies: experiments, major updates, new projects, interesting findings
3. Drafts content in `promptengines/experiments/` or `promptengines/updates/`
4. You review + approve → publish

### lab.promptengines.com Content
1. You send Andy a source article or topic
2. Andy drafts in `lab/queue/` → moves to `lab/drafts/` when writing starts
3. You review → moves to `lab/published/` when live
