# StoryBook Studio — Influencer & PR Outreach

Last updated: 2026-02-26

---

## Strategy Overview

**Goal:** Get 10–20 pieces of authentic UGC content from real parents before paid ads
**Approach:** Free books in exchange for honest reviews/content
**Target:** Micro-influencers (5k–100k followers) — higher trust, better engagement, lower cost

---

## Influencer Target Profiles

### Tier 1 — Parenting Micro-Influencers
- Mom/Dad bloggers with 10k–100k followers
- Content: "what I got my kid", "honest reviews", "toddler activities"
- Platforms: TikTok, Instagram Reels
- What we offer: Free digital book + free print copy
- What we ask: 1 post/reel/TikTok, honest review, tag us

### Tier 2 — Gift/Lifestyle Influencers
- "Gift guide" accounts, "things I love" creators
- Content: gift roundups, product recommendations
- Platform: Instagram, Pinterest, YouTube
- What we offer: Free print book
- What we ask: Feature in a gift guide or dedicated post

### Tier 3 — Educators/Teachers
- Teacher TikTok community ("TeacherTok")
- Homeschool parents on YouTube/Instagram
- What we offer: Free classroom set of digital books (5 personalized)
- What we ask: Video showing kids' reactions
