# StoryBook Studio — Paid Ads Strategy

**Site:** https://storybookstudio.promptengines.com
**Last updated:** 2026-02-26

---

## Overview

Paid ads are Phase 4 — we run them AFTER we have:
- ✅ Proven messaging (from organic content)
- ✅ Conversion data (what % of visitors buy)
- ✅ At least some UGC/social proof to use as ad creative
- ✅ Pixel/tracking installed on site

This order matters. Running ads to a cold, unoptimized page burns money. Organic first, paid to scale.

## Platform Breakdown

---

### 📘 Meta Ads (Facebook + Instagram)
**Priority:** 🔴 #1 paid channel
**Why:** Best targeting for parents. Massive audience. Visual product = perfect for feed/Reels.

#### Audiences
| Audience | Type | Notes |
|----------|------|-------|
| Parents of children aged 2–8 | Interest | Facebook's parenting segments |
| Gift buyers — kids | Behavior | "Engaged shoppers" + gift-giving behavior |
| Wonderbly / personalized book buyers | Interest | Direct competitor audience |
| Lookalike — email list | LAL 1–3% | Once we have 500+ emails |
| Lookalike — purchasers | LAL 1% | Once we have 100+ buyers |
| Retargeting — site visitors | Custom | Anyone who visited but didn't buy |
| Retargeting — add to cart | Custom | Highest intent, discount offer |
| Grandparents (55+) buying for grandkids | Age + interest | Gifting persona, high AOV |

#### Ad Formats
| Format | Use Case |
|--------|---------|
| Reels / Video | Reaction videos, book creation screen record — best CTR |
| Carousel | Show multiple book page spreads |
| Static image | Gift angle — book with a bow, clean product shot |
| Story | Short punchy hook, swipe up to create |

#### Campaign Structure
```
Campaign 1 — Awareness (TOF)
  └── Ad Set: Parents 25–40, interests: parenting, children's books, gifts
      └── Creative: Reaction video / "watch me make this in 3 min"

Campaign 2 — Consideration (MOF)
  └── Ad Set: Engaged visitors (viewed 50%+ of video or visited site)
      └── Creative: How it works, social proof, testimonials

Campaign 3 — Conversion (BOF)
  └── Ad Set: Add-to-cart abandoners, email list
      └── Creative: Urgency, discount, "finish what you started"

Campaign 4 — Gift Buyers
  └── Ad Set: Age 45–65, grandparent interests, gift behavior
      └── Creative: "The gift they'll never forget" angle
```

#### Budget
| Phase | Daily Budget | Notes |
|-------|-------------|-------|
| Test | $15–20/day | 2 weeks, test 3–4 creatives |
| Scale winners | $50–100/day | Double down on ROAS > 2x |
| Full scale | $200+/day | After proven funnel |

#### KPIs
- CPC target: < $1.50
- CPM target: < $15
- CTR target: > 1.5%
- ROAS target: > 2x (break even), > 3x (scale)

### 🎵 TikTok Ads
**Priority:** 🔴 #1 for creative testing, #2 for spend
**Why:** Parenting content is massive on TikTok. Organic-style ads perform best. Lower CPMs than Meta.

#### Ad Formats
| Format | Use Case |
|--------|---------|
| In-Feed Ads | Looks like organic TikTok — highest performance |
| Spark Ads | Boost your own organic posts that are already performing |
| TopView | Brand awareness — expensive, skip for now |

#### Creative Strategy
TikTok rewards content that looks and feels native. Our best-performing ads will look exactly like organic posts.

**Top creative concepts:**
1. **The Reaction** — Parent shows child the finished book, captures genuine reaction
2. **The Creation** — Screen record of making the book in real time, satisfying to watch
3. **The Reveal** — Book pages flipping, child's face illustrated, emotional music
4. **The Problem/Solution** — "I always struggle finding gifts for my kid's birthday — until I found this"
5. **The Comparison** — Side by side: hours on Amazon vs. 3 minutes on StoryBook Studio

#### Audiences
| Audience | Notes |
|----------|-------|
| Parents of toddlers | Primary |
| Gift shoppers | Behavioral |
| Lookalike — website visitors | Pixel-based |
| Interest: children's books, parenting, AI tools | Layered |

#### Budget
| Phase | Daily Budget |
|-------|-------------|
| Test | $20–30/day |
| Scale | $75–150/day |

#### KPIs
- CPM target: < $10 (TikTok typically cheaper than Meta)
- CTR target: > 1%
- CPA target: < $15 (cost per book created/purchased)

**Priority:** 🟡 Secondary — brand awareness + AI community
**Why:** The AI/tech crowd is on X. "AI children's book" is a topic that spreads here. Not a direct-response channel for this product — more for brand building and press pickups.

#### Strategy
- Promoted posts targeting: AI enthusiasts, parenting journalists, tech parents
- Keyword targeting: "AI art", "personalized gifts", "children's books", "parenting"
- Follower lookalike: Target followers of Wonderbly, parenting influencers, AI accounts

#### Ad Types
- Promoted tweets — share reaction videos, product demos
- Website clicks — drive to landing page during gift seasons

#### Budget
- Keep low: $5–10/day
- Spike during gift seasons (Christmas, Mother's Day, Father's Day)

#### KPIs
- Impressions + engagement rate (awareness metric, not direct response)
- Brand mention growth

## Creative Strategy (Across All Platforms)

### What Works for This Product
1. **Authentic reactions** — real kids seeing their book for the first time
2. **Creation process** — satisfying to watch, builds curiosity
3. **Emotional storytelling** — "this is why I built this" founder content
4. **Gift positioning** — before/after of gift-giving experience
5. **Speed angle** — "I made this in 3 minutes" is a pattern interrupt

### What to Avoid
- Overly polished/corporate ads
- Leading with "AI" in headline
- Generic "best gift for kids" messaging without demonstration
- Long-form video (keep under 30s for paid, under 15s for TikTok/Reels)

### Creative Testing Framework
Every 2 weeks, test:
- 2 hooks (first 3 seconds)
- 2 visuals (reaction vs. creation process)
- 2 CTAs ("Create your book" vs. "Make it now")

Kill losers at $50 spend. Scale winners to $100+/day.
