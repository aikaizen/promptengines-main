# StoryBook Studio — In-Person & Events Strategy

**Last updated:** 2026-02-26

---

## Why In-Person

For a product like StoryBook Studio, nothing converts like a live demo. When a parent sees their child's name appear in an illustrated story in real time, in front of them — that's a sale. Events also generate:

- UGC content (photos, videos at the booth)
- Email captures
- Word of mouth in tight-knit local communities
- Local press coverage
- Real user feedback on the product

---

## Austin — Primary Market

Austin is home base. It's a perfect proving ground:
- 2M+ metro, fast-growing
- Heavy tech/startup culture = early adopters
- Strong family communities in suburbs (Cedar Park, Round Rock, Pflugerville, Bee Cave)
- World-class events: SXSW, Texas Book Festival
- Active local press and parenting media

## Austin Event Calendar

### 🔴 Tier 1 — Must Do

#### Texas Book Festival
- **When:** October (annually)
- **Where:** Texas State Capitol grounds, Austin
- **Audience:** 40,000+ attendees, book lovers, families, educators
- **Why it's perfect:** Literal book audience. Parents + kids everywhere. Press coverage.
- **Our play:** Booth with live demo station. "Create your child's book in 3 minutes" live. Print samples on display. QR for digital purchase. Offer festival discount.
- **Action:** Apply for exhibitor booth in spring for October festival

#### SXSW (South by Southwest)
- **When:** March (annually)
- **Where:** Austin Convention Center + venues citywide
- **Audience:** Tech media, investors, early adopters, global press
- **Why it's relevant:** AI + kids + creativity = SXSW editorial story. Press pickup potential is massive.
- **Our play:** Not a booth — a *story*. Pitch to SXSW EDU track. Host a side event ("AI and the Future of Children's Storytelling"). Get featured in SXSW blog/newsletter.
- **Action:** Submit SXSW EDU panel proposal (deadline typically September for March)

---

### 🟡 Tier 2 — High Value

#### BookPeople (Austin's Premier Indie Bookstore)
- **When:** Ongoing — monthly events
- **Where:** 603 N Lamar Blvd, Austin
- **Audience:** Book lovers, parents, kids, educators
- **Our play:** Partner for a "Create Your Child's Story" event night. BookPeople hosts author/creator events regularly — we pitch ourselves as a creator event. Live demo + order station.
- **Action:** Email events coordinator at BookPeople

#### Austin Public Library — Storytime Events
- **When:** Ongoing — weekly storytimes across 20 branches
- **Audience:** Parents + kids 0–8, exact target demographic
- **Our play:** Partner with Austin Public Library for a "Make Your Child the Hero" storytime session. We demo the app, kids see their name in a story, parents sign up.
- **Action:** Contact Austin Public Library programming department

#### Austin Farmers Markets
- **SFC Farmers Market** (Downtown + Mueller) — Saturdays
- **Sunset Valley Farmers Market** — Saturdays
- **Audience:** Families, parents, weekend shoppers
- **Our play:** Pop-up table with iPad demo. "Create a free page for your child right now." Email capture. Print sample to take home.
- **Frequency:** Monthly presence, spike before gift seasons

#### Austin ISD School Events
- **What:** Back-to-school nights, book fairs, parent nights
- **Audience:** Parents, teachers, school librarians
- **Our play:** Partner with school libraries for reading program tie-in. Offer teacher/classroom discount. Appear at book fairs.

---

### 🟢 Tier 3 — Seasonal / Supplemental

#### Holiday Gift Fairs
- **When:** November–December
- **Where:** Domain Northside, Barton Creek Square, The Arboretum
- **Our play:** Holiday pop-up booth. "Give the gift of their own story." Gift card sales. Print orders for Christmas delivery.

#### Austin City Limits Music Festival (ACL)
- **When:** October
- **Audience:** Families (family weekend)
- **Our play:** Not a booth — street marketing near venue. QR flyers. Guerrilla sampling.

#### Kendra Scott / Local Boutique Partnerships
- **What:** Austin has dozens of family/gift boutiques
- **Our play:** Leave printed sample books + QR cards on consignment or as display items

#### Mom/Dad Influencer Meetups
- **What:** Austin has active parenting influencer community
- **Our play:** Host or sponsor a small "parents night out" demo event. 20–30 local influencers, free books, photos

## Event Marketing Kit

Everything needed at any event:

| Item | Purpose | Status |
|------|---------|--------|
| Printed sample books (3–5) | Physical demo, people can hold | ⬜ Need to produce |
| iPad/tablet with app loaded | Live creation demo | ⬜ Need setup |
| QR code stand / banner | Drive to site | ⬜ Need to design |
| 1-page flyer with QR | Takeaway | ⬜ Need to design |
| Email capture form | List building | ⬜ Need to set up |
| Business cards | Press/partner networking | ⬜ Need to design |
| Event discount code | Conversion incentive | ⬜ Need to create |
| Social handles | Easy to remember + display | ⬜ Need accounts first |

### The Live Demo Script
1. "Can I show you something cool? [Pull up app on iPad]"
2. "What's your child's name?" [Type it in]
3. "Watch this..." [Generate a page with their name]
4. "That took 10 seconds. The full book takes about 3 minutes."
5. "Available as a digital download or we print and ship it. Perfect gift."
6. "Scan this QR code — I'll give you [X]% off today."
7. Capture email at QR landing page
