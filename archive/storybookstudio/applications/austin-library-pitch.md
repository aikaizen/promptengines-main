# Austin Public Library — Partnership Pitch

**Website:** https://library.austintexas.gov
**Programming contact:** Via online form at library.austintexas.gov/programs
**Branches:** 20 branches across Austin metro
**Existing programs:** Weekly storytimes, family reading programs, summer reading challenges

---

## Partnership Concept

**Program Name:** "Be the Hero of Your Story" — StoryBook Studio Storytime

**Format:**
- Partner with existing storytime programs at APL branches
- Librarian reads a sample StoryBook Studio book during storytime
- We provide a demo station — kids type in their name, see it appear in a story
- Free digital sample page for every attending family
- Print books available for purchase via QR code

**Why APL should say yes:**
- Enhances existing programming with zero cost to them
- Supports APL's mission of making reading engaging for young children
- Especially powerful for reluctant readers — seeing yourself in a story = instant engagement
- We provide all materials, they provide the audience

**Offer to APL:**
- Free digital books for all library branches (one per branch for display)
- Donation of 5 printed books to APL children's collection
- We'll promote the event to our audience, driving foot traffic to their branches
