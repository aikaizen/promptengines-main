## StoryBook Studio
*storybookstudio.promptengines.com*

AI-powered personalized children's storybooks. Parents describe their child — name, look, personality — and the app generates a fully illustrated storybook starring them. 34+ art styles. Digital download or print-on-demand.

The thing we keep hearing from parents: their kid goes quiet when they see their name on the cover. That moment is what we built this for.

**Status:** Live.

---

## Kaizen
*kaizen.promptengines.com*

An education app for kids with AI-powered games built in. Numbers, battles, imagination — the kind of learning that doesn't feel like learning. Most recently: a Math Battle mode and a complete architectural overhaul that makes it possible to add new games without touching the core app.

**Status:** Active development. Shipping fast.

---

## Flow
*flow.promptengines.com*

An AI tutor. Students get a home screen with 20 lessons, an AI that adapts to how they're doing, and challenge cards that appear once they've warmed up. The goal is to make the AI feel like a patient tutor, not a pushy one — which means sometimes it says nothing and lets the student think.

Latest experiment: we removed the "nudge" system that injected motivational prompts every few turns. Turns out students work better without it.

**Status:** Active development.

## Lab

## The Method

Every product in the ecosystem is built by humans and AI agents working together. The commit logs tell the story — Claude co-authors most of our code. We're not hiding that. We think it's the most interesting thing about how we work.

Building in public means showing the failures too. Watch the repos.
