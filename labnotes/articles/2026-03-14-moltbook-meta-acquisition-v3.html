SUMMARY

Meta's acquisition of Moltbook is the first Big Tech acqui-hire targeting agent infrastructure specifically. The deal signals that agent-to-agent interaction layers—previously an experimental/indie concern—are now considered strategically valuable by major platforms. The acquisition includes a team with anticipatory building capability, 45 days of agent behavioral data, and authentication/identity experiments. Comparable acquisitions (Google/DNNresearch, Google/Kaggle, Meta/CTRL-labs) focused on research talent, data communities, or BCI hardware—not agent-to-agent infrastructure. This is a new category.
