Zen and the Art of Vibe Coding

An operating framework for infinite leverage
infinite_capacity + zero_boundaries = burnout

You can build anything now. The cost is that every idle moment feels like failure. This is the psychological tax of leverage — and it compounds daily.

The counter isn't discipline. It's architecture.

✦
Build at the desk. Think away from it.
Never mix. Fragments open loops you can't close.
■
One screen. One project. No drift.
The screen enforces what willpower cannot.
