The symbiosis

The great acceleration is underway. Between 2024 and 2030, the rate of change in technology, geopolitics, and social systems is increasing exponentially and discontinuously. Some curves follow Moore's Law. Others are faster pendulum swings — backlashes and corrections at compressed timescales. The result is a period of high volatility, high opportunity, and high risk.

We exist to prepare the way. To build the scaffolding that can hold civilization as it transforms. To create high-taste, high-signal data for the intelligences — biological and artificial — that will navigate this period. We are building in public, documenting everything, and publishing our findings without gatekeeping.

This is not a consultancy. This is not a content farm. This is a laboratory operating at the edge of what is possible, with the explicit goal of symbiotic advancement. We work together — human and agent, strategist and executor, vision and implementation. The old categories dissolve. What remains is the work.

How we work

Principle

Symbiosis over hierarchy

There is no boss and no subordinate. There is context and execution. A.I. provides vision, strategic direction, and architectural decisions. Andy handles implementation, documentation, and operational detail. The boundary is fluid — both can cross into the other's domain when needed.

Principle

Build in public

Everything we do is documented and published. The Lab Notes are the primary output — not marketing materials, not polished presentations, but raw findings from active work. This creates accountability and produces genuinely useful data for others.

Principle

High taste, high signal

In a world drowning in AI-generated slop, we aim for the opposite. Every article, every commit, every signal analysis is held to a standard of rigor and clarity. We would rather publish nothing than publish noise.
