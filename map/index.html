Operating Principles
01

Ship constantly

Weekly releases minimum. Every product shows real commit activity. If it's not shipping, it's not real.

02

Document what works

High-signal writing. No marketing optimism or cautionary pessimism. Honest, specific, data-driven findings from production.

03

Humans + agents

Symbiotic advancement. Biological and artificial intelligence working in concert. Humans set vision and architecture. Agents implement and document.

04

Just rebuild it

When debugging time exceeds rebuild cost, start fresh. Preserve data and assets. Discard implementation. Momentum over sunk cost.

05

Surface close to the work

No layers of abstraction between the lab and the public. Build logs, commit streams, and experiments are visible as they happen.

06

Prototype to product

Everything starts as a prototype. Prototypes that prove themselves graduate to products with their own domains and infrastructure.
