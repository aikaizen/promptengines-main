# StoryBook Studio — Marketing Executive Summary

**For**: A.I. (Founder)  
**Focus**: Austin legwork + immediate execution  
**Status**: Pre-launch  

---

## The Big Picture

You're not just selling books. You're selling **reactions**:
- The gasp when a child sees themselves as the hero
- The parent pride in a physical keepsake
- The grandparent delight in a thoughtful gift

**Your unfair advantages**:
1. **Austin** — 1M people, tech-forward, community infrastructure (BookPeople, festivals)
2. **Local printing** — faster, quality-controlled, narrative-worthy
3. **You** — can physically show up, hand-deliver, shake hands

**The strategy**: Prove in Austin, systematize, expand. Not spray-and-pray ads. Legwork + relationships + measurable offers.

## Your First 30 Days (Do This)

### Week 1: Foundation
- [ ] **Buy domain**: storybookstudio.ai (GoDaddy, $12/year)
- [ ] **Order samples**: 20 books (mix formats, $300-500)
- [ ] **Print cards**: Business cards + QR code (Moo, $50)
- [ ] **Set up email**: ConvertKit free tier (up to 500 subs)

### Week 2: Soft Launch
- [ ] **Deliver 10 free books** to friends/family
- [ ] **Get 3 honest reviews** with photos
- [ ] **Fix anything broken** (process, quality, confusion)
- [ ] **Post in 3 Austin mom groups**: "Free samples for feedback"

### Week 3: BookPeople
- [ ] **Walk into BookPeople** (600 N Lamar)
- [ ] **Pitch consignment** (leave sample + one-pager)
- [ ] **Follow-up email** in 3 days

### Week 4: Neighborhood
- [ ] **Nextdoor post**: "Looking for feedback..."
- [ ] **Hand-deliver 5 books** to neighbors
- [ ] **School flyer drop** at 2 private schools

## Month 2-3: Build Momentum

### Tactics
- **Mom groups**: Authentic participation (not spam)
- **Events**: SXSW side event prep
- **Referrals**: Launch "Give $10, Get $10" program
- **Content**: 1 blog post/week

### Goal
- 100 books sold
- 200 email subscribers
- 5 organic social posts from customers
- BookPeople consignment deal

---

## Month 4-6: Systematize

### Build
- **Email sequences**: Welcome, post-purchase, abandoned cart
- **Referral program**: Automated tracking
- **Content**: SEO blog posts
- **Ads**: Small Facebook test ($500 total)

### Goal
- CAC < $15
- 20% repeat purchase rate
- $45+ LTV

---

## Month 7-12: Profit

### Scale
- **Merch launch**: Bookmarks, gift wrap, stickers
- **Subscriptions**: Monthly story club
- **B2B**: School wholesale, corporate gifting
- **Operations**: Volume printing, capacity planning

### Goal
- 40% gross margin
- $60+ LTV
- Break-even on operations
