## Phase 0 — Fix the Website First (Before ANY Marketing)
*Nothing else matters until there's something to land on.*

### 🔴 Do These Before Spending $1 or Attending Any Event

- [ ] **Buy a standalone domain** — `storybookstudio.ai` recommended. Set up redirect from current URL.
- [ ] **Build a marketing landing page** — Hero → How It Works → Sample Books → Pricing → Testimonials → FAQ → CTA. Must exist before any ads run or events happen.
- [ ] **Rewrite hero copy** — Lead with emotion, not features. "Every child deserves to be the hero of their own story" not "generate artwork in 34+ art styles"
- [ ] **Add visible pricing** — Users need to know print costs before they emotionally invest
- [ ] **Add email capture** — Lead magnet ("see a free sample page") on landing page
- [ ] **Add onboarding flow** — New users need a "Quick mode" guided path, not a blank canvas

### 🟡 Do These in Week 1–2

- [ ] **Add trust signals** — Even fake-it-till-you-make-it: "X books created", "loved by parents in 20+ countries", parent quotes
- [ ] **Add FAQ section** — "Is it free?", "How does print work?", "How long does shipping take?", "Is my child's data safe?"
- [ ] **Set up blog** — Even 3 posts gets Google indexing started
- [ ] **Mobile audit** — Test the full creation flow on iPhone. Fix anything broken.
- [ ] **Add privacy/safety statement** — Parents are handing over info about their kids. Reassure them.

## The Product

An AI-powered app that creates personalized, illustrated children's storybooks starring your child as the main character. Available as a digital download and print-on-demand. Ready in minutes.

**Core audiences:**
- Parents of kids aged 2–8
- Gift-givers (grandparents, aunts/uncles, family friends)
- Educators (teachers, homeschool parents, librarians)

**Competitive edge:** AI-generated unique illustrations + digital + print + minutes, not weeks.

## Phase 5 — Paid Acquisition (Week 6+)

**Goal:** Scale proven organic channels with paid. Every platform has a role.

### Full strategy → `06-paid-ads-strategy.md`

### Platform Summary

| Platform | Role | Start Budget | When to Start |
|----------|------|-------------|--------------|
| **Meta (FB + IG)** | Primary — parent targeting | $15–20/day | After UGC ready |
| **TikTok** | Primary — creative testing | $20–30/day | After organic proven |
| **Google Ads** | Bottom-funnel capture | $20–30/day | Anytime |
| **Pinterest** | Gift seasons | $10/day (spike 5x at Christmas) | After Pinterest account built |
| **X / Twitter** | Brand awareness, AI press | $5–10/day | Ongoing |

### Pre-Spend Checklist (Do Before Spending $1)
- [ ] All pixels installed + firing correctly
- [ ] Conversion events tracked (book created, purchase)
- [ ] At least 3 UGC creatives ready
- [ ] Landing page converting > 3% organically
- [ ] Retargeting audiences seeded

### Creative Strategy
- Lead with reaction videos (child sees book for first time)
- Creation process content (watch it happen in 3 minutes)
- Gift positioning (before/after)
- Keep hooks under 3 seconds, full video under 30s
- Test 2 hooks + 2 visuals every 2 weeks, kill losers at $50 spend
