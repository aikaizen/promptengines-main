## PRODUCT QUESTIONS

### What is StoryBook Studio?
A personalized children's book service. You upload a photo of your child, customize their character, choose a story, and we create a book where they're the main character. Printed and shipped to you.

### How is this different from other personalized book services?
Three main differences:

1. **Character consistency**: The same face on every page, not just the cover. We built architecture specifically for this.

2. **34 art styles**: From photoreal to pixel art, you choose the aesthetic that fits your child.

3. **Made in Austin**: We print locally, not overseas. Faster turnaround, quality control, local economic impact.

### What ages is this for?
Primarily 2-8 years old, but we've seen engagement from 18 months up to 10 years. The key is matching story complexity and art style to developmental stage.

### Do you ship outside the US?
Yes, but shipping times and costs vary. US orders: 3-5 days. International: 7-14 days depending on location.

### What's the quality like?
Professional book quality. Three formats:
- **Saddle stitch**: 20-30 pages, stapled, casual use
- **Paperback**: 30-40 pages, perfect bound, standard durability
- **Hardcover**: 40+ pages, case bound, archival paper, keepsake quality

Paper stock: 100# gloss (saddle), 80# matte (paperback), archival uncoated (hardcover). 300 DPI printing.

## PROCESS QUESTIONS

### How does the character builder work?
1. Upload one clear photo of your child
2. Our AI extracts facial features, hair color, skin tone
3. You customize: hair style, clothing, expression, accessories
4. Preview instantly — adjust until it looks right
5. The character stays consistent across the entire book

### What makes a good upload photo?
- Clear, well-lit face
- Looking at camera or slightly to side
- Neutral or slight smile expression
- Recent (within 6 months for young kids)
- No sunglasses, hats, or heavy shadows

### Can I use multiple photos?
For the character builder: one main photo. But you can preview with different photos to see which extracts best.

For the book: you can add additional photos to the dedication page if you want.

### How long does it take?
- **Preview creation**: Instant (10-30 seconds)
- **Digital book**: Same day delivery (PDF)
- **Printed book**: 3-5 days production + shipping
- **Rush option**: 2-3 days (additional cost)

### What if I don't like the preview?
Adjust and re-preview. Change hair, clothing, expression, or try a different art style. It's free to preview. You only pay when you order print.

### Can I see a physical sample before ordering?
Yes. Email us at hello@storybookstudio.ai and request a sample. We'll send a small format book with generic content so you can feel the paper and binding quality.

## PRIVACY & DATA

### What happens to the photo I upload?
We use it to generate the character. That's it. Specifically:

- Photo is processed securely (encrypted in transit and at rest)
- We don't use your photos to train AI models
- We don't sell or share photos with third parties
- You can delete your account and all associated data anytime
- We retain order records for business/legal purposes, but photos can be deleted on request

### Is this safe for children's data?
We comply with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act). We don't collect more data than necessary, we don't market to children, and parents control all account functions.

### Who can see the books I create?
Only you. Books are private by default. If you choose to share (social media, email), that's your decision.

## TROUBLESHOOTING

### The character doesn't look like my kid. What do I do?
1. Try adjusting hair, clothing, expression in the builder
2. Try a different art style (some styles render more accurately)
3. Try a different source photo
4. If none of that works, email us with specifics. We'll troubleshoot or refund.

### The preview won't load.
Try:
- Refreshing the page
- Using a different browser (Chrome works best)
- Checking your internet connection
- Clearing browser cache

If still broken, email us.

### I can't upload my photo.
Check:
- File size under 10MB
- Format is JPG or PNG
- File isn't corrupted (can you open it normally?)

If issues persist, email us the photo and we'll upload manually.

### The website is broken on my phone.
We work on mobile, but the character builder is easier on desktop/tablet. If you're having issues, try from a computer or email us.
