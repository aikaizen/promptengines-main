**One-Pager Contents**:

- Proof: Sample book (leave behind)
- Terms: 20% commission, net-30, easy reorders
- Contact: Your name, phone, email

#### Tactic 2: Austin Mom Facebook Groups
**Groups to Target**:
- Austin Moms (50K+ members)
- Austin Area Moms
- Central Austin Parents
- Homeschooling Austin

**Your Action**:
- Join as yourself (not the brand)
- Participate authentically for 1 week (answer questions, be helpful)
- Week 2: Post offering free samples for feedback

**Post Template** (Anti-Slop Checked):
```
Hi! I'm [Name], an Austin parent building something I wish existed. 

StoryBook Studio creates personalized children's books where your kid 
is the main character. Upload a photo, customize the character, choose 
a story, get a printed book in 3-5 days.

I'm looking for 5 Austin families to try it free in exchange for 
honest feedback. Not looking for fluff — tell me what breaks, what 
confuses you, what your kid actually thinks.

If interested, comment or DM. Local only for now (I want to hand-deliver 
and talk in person).
```

**Follow-up**:
- Hand-deliver books
- 3 days later: "How did it go?"
- 7 days later: "Would you recommend this to a friend?"
- If yes: "Can I ask you to post your experience in this group? No pressure."

## Phase 2: ACQUIRE (Months 4-6)
**Goal**: Systematize customer acquisition  
**Budget**: $2,000 (email tool, small ad tests, print inventory)  
**Success Metrics**: CAC < $15, LTV > $45, 20% repeat

---

### Email System (The Engine)

**Tool**: ConvertKit or Mailchimp ($29/month)

**Sequences**:

#### Welcome Series (New Subscriber)
1. **Immediate**: Thank you + 50% off code `WELCOME-50-NEW-PERP`
2. **Day 3**: How it works (character builder demo)
3. **Day 7**: Texas pride angle ("Made in Austin")
4. **Day 14**: Social proof (real reviews from Phase 1)
5. **Day 21**: Last chance ("Code expires in 48 hours")

#### Post-Purchase Series (New Customer)
1. **Immediate**: Order confirmation + expected delivery
2. **Day 3 (book arrives)**: "How did it look? Reply with thoughts"
3. **Day 7**: Review request ("Would you recommend?")
4. **Day 14**: Referral ask ("Share with friends, you both get $10")
5. **Day 30**: Second book offer ("Remember the character we built?")

#### Abandoned Cart
1. **1 hour**: "Forgot something?"
2. **24 hours**: "Still interested? Here's 10% off" `SAVE-10-CART-24H`
3. **72 hours**: "Last chance: 15% off expires tonight" `SAVE-15-LAST-24H`

---

### Referral Program (Viral Engine)

**Mechanic**: Give $10, Get $10

**How It Works**:
- Customer gets unique code: `REF-[NAME]-20`
- Friend gets 20% off (their $10)
- Customer gets $10 credit when friend buys

**Promotion**:
- In-book insert: "Share with a friend, you both save"
- Post-purchase email
- Order confirmation page

**Target**: 30% of customers refer at least one person

**Budget**: $5,000 (inventory, tools, ops)

### Operational Scaling

#### Printing Capacity
**Current**: Local printer, 1-5 books/day  
**Scale Target**: 50 books/day  

**Options**:
1. **Negotiate volume discount** with current printer (print cost ↓ 15%)
2. **Second printer** (redundancy + capacity)
3. **In-house investment** (if volume justifies $10K equipment)

**Decision Point**: Month 9, if >30 books/week consistently → invest in capacity

---

### Merch Launch

#### Phase 1: Book Accessories (Month 7)
| Item | Cost | Price | Why It Works |
|------|------|-------|--------------|
| Bookmark | $0.50 | $4 | Upsell at checkout, low decision cost |
| Gift wrap | $1 | $6 | Occasion purchases (birthdays) |
| Gift bag | $2 | $8 | Premium positioning |

**Strategy**: Offer as bundles at checkout
- "Complete the gift: Add bookmark + wrap for $8" (save $2)
- Attach rate goal: 40%

---

#### Phase 2: Character Merch (Month 9)
| Item | Cost | Price | Production |
|------|------|-------|------------|
| Sticker sheet | $1 | $6 | Local print shop |
| T-shirt | $8 | $24 | Print-on-demand (no inventory risk) |
| Tote bag | $4 | $18 | Local screen printer |

**Strategy**: "Your kid's character on a shirt" — emotional hook
**Fulfillment**: Print-on-demand for clothing (no risk), local for stickers (fast)
