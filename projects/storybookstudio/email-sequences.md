## EMAIL BEST PRACTICES

### Subject Lines
• Keep under 50 characters when possible
• Front-load important words
• Avoid ALL CAPS and excessive punctuation
• Test: would YOU open this?

### Send Timing
• Tuesday-Thursday, 10am or 2pm (local time)
• Avoid Monday mornings and Friday afternoons
• Consider parent schedules (after school drop-off, after bedtime)

### Personalization
• Use [First Name] when you have it
• Reference specific book/child when possible
• Segment by behavior (purchasers vs. previewers vs. subscribers)

### Testing
• A/B test subject lines (20% of list each, winner to remaining 60%)
• Test send times by segment
• Monitor open rates, click rates, unsubscribe rates
• Kill any sequence underperforming (open < 20%, click < 2%)

### Compliance
• Unsubscribe link in every email
• Physical address in footer
• Honor unsubscribe within 24 hours
• No purchased lists — only organic signups
