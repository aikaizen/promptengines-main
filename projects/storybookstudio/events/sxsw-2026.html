What This Is

An informal demo day during SXSW week. Not an official SXSW event. No badge required. No pitch deck. Just a working session where we show what we've built, answer questions, and get feedback from people who actually understand AI.

Format: Open house. Drop in anytime 2-6 PM.
Capacity: Limited to 50 people.
Cost: Free. No SXSW badge required.

Who Should Attend

Founders building AI products. Parents curious about AI-generated content. Engineers interested in computer vision + print. Investors who want to see something working before hearing a pitch. Anyone who wants to hold a physical AI-generated book and decide if it's real.

Not a networking event. Not a party. A working session with demos, technical discussion, and honest Q&A. We'll show you exactly how the product works, what's broken, and what we're fixing next.

You'll leave with a printed sample book (if you bring a photo) and a clear understanding of whether this technology is ready for real use.
