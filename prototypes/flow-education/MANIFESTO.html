How We Build

Continuous Testing

Every interaction is an assessment. We don't wait for quizzes—we learn from every tap, every pause, every smile. The system adapts in real-time, adjusting difficulty, pacing, and approach based on what works for this child.

Continuous Improvement

The system gets better every day. Not just new content, but refined delivery, improved scaffolding, smarter hints. Like a great teacher who learns from every student, our tutors evolve.

Mastery Before Progression

We celebrate effort and reward understanding. Children don't advance because time passed— they advance because they've got it. This builds confidence that lasts a lifetime.

Teacher-Respecting Design

Built for real classrooms. One teacher, 25 students, limited time. Setup in under 2 minutes. Works offline. No interruptions, no troubleshooting, just learning that flows.

The Journey

Phase 0 — MVP

Prove the Concept

Kindergarten letters A-E, number 1. Six 6-minute lessons. Goal: 80% of children complete 60 minutes independently. Teacher satisfaction 4+/5. Learn what works.

Phase 1 — Expansion

Build the Curriculum

Teachers create lessons. 10,000 students using weekly. Reading and math foundations. The system learns from thousands of learners, improving for everyone.

Phase 2 — Validation

Prove the Impact

Measurable learning gains vs. control groups. Tutors trusted by teachers. Data shows what we believe: personalized attention at scale works.

Phase 3 — Scale

Every Child Deserves a Tutor

Comprehensive K-12 curriculum. Reading, math, science, arts. Every child has a personal guide who knows exactly what they need.
