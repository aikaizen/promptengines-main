The Four Phases

Phase 0 — Now

Kindergarten Chrome tablets. 10 quests. Basic adaptive logic. Validate the 60-minute standard.

Phase 2 — Agentic

Phase 3 — Knowledge

Full K-12 curriculum. Knowledge graphs. Agentic asset creation. Every student has a personal tutor.

How Flow Works

Active Engagement

Touch, trace, listen, find. Kids do the work—tapping letters, drawing shapes, solving puzzles. Not watching videos.

Adaptive Path

Stuck on a letter? Extra practice appears. Mastered it? The next challenge unlocks. Every child moves at their own pace.

Independent by Design

No teacher intervention required. Clear audio instructions. Visual progress cues. Kids know what to do next.

Real Outcomes

Recognize A-E. Count to 10. Trace letters with accuracy. Measurable skills, not just screen time.
