The Problem

Khan Academy Kids entertains for 10 minutes, then kids zone out. Duolingo ABC drills letters until boredom. ABCmouse uses slot-machine mechanics to addict children to "learning."

None of them work in real classrooms. Teachers have 25 students. If the app requires hand-holding, it's useless.

The Four Phases

Phase 0 — Now

Kindergarten Chrome tablets. 10 quests. Basic adaptive logic. Validate the 60-minute standard.

Phase 1 — Tools

Teacher lesson creation. Chat-based LLM tutors. Middle school pilot. Asset marketplace.

Phase 2 — Agentic

Intelligent asset surfacing. Scoring and recommendations. Student data system.

Phase 3 — Knowledge

Full K-12 curriculum. Knowledge graphs. Agentic asset creation. Every student has a personal tutor.

What We Refuse

Refuse: Edutainment

90% play, 10% learning. Cartoons with math problems. Not good enough.

Refuse: Gamification

Points, badges, streaks. Addiction mechanics exploiting dopamine. We refuse.

Refuse: Passive

Videos that play while kids stare. Adaptive speed, not adaptive learning.

Refuse: Babysitting

Apps that require constant teacher help. We build self-sufficient tutors.
