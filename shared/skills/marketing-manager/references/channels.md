# Marketing Channels Reference

## Paid Social

### Meta (Facebook + Instagram)
- Best for: Parent targeting, visual products, gift buyers
- Ad formats: Reels, Carousel, Static, Stories
- Audiences: Interest-based parents, lookalikes, retargeting
- Pixel: Must be installed before spending
- KPIs: CPC < $1.50, CTR > 1.5%, ROAS > 2x

### TikTok
- Best for: Organic-style creative testing, younger parents, viral potential
- Ad formats: In-Feed, Spark Ads (boost organic), TopView (expensive)
- Creative rule: Must look native — polished = bad
- KPIs: CPM < $10, CPA < $15

### Pinterest
- Best for: Gift seasons, high-AOV buyers, female 25–45
- Ad formats: Promoted Pins, Video Pins, Shopping Pins
- Seasonal: Spike budget at Christmas (5x), Mother's Day (3x), Father's Day (2x)
- Note: Search-intent platform — keyword targeting is critical

### X / Twitter
- Best for: Brand awareness, AI/tech press, journalist pickup
- Not a direct-response channel for consumer products
- Keep budget minimal ($5–10/day), spike during launches

### Google Ads
- Best for: Bottom-funnel, high-intent searchers
- Campaign types: Search (exact match), Performance Max, Display Retargeting
- KPIs: CTR > 5%, CPC < $2, ROAS > 3x

## Organic Social

### TikTok Organic
- Hook in first 3 seconds is everything
- Best formats: Reaction videos, creation process, problem/solution
- Post 3–5x/week for algorithm traction

### Instagram
- Reels > Feed posts for reach
- Stories for engagement/polls
- Shopping tags on product imagery
- Hashtag sets: 5–10 per post, mix niche + broad

### Pinterest Organic
- Evergreen platform — pins drive traffic for months/years
- Create boards around gifting, parenting, stories, education
- Repin actively, keyword-optimize all descriptions

### Facebook Groups
- Seed genuinely — don't spam
- Target: parenting groups, local mom groups, gift idea groups
- One authentic post can drive hundreds of visits

## Email Marketing

### Platform Options
- Klaviyo — best for ecommerce, powerful segmentation
- ConvertKit — creator-friendly, good automation
- Mailchimp — free tier, good for starting out

### Sequence Types
- Welcome (3 emails): What it is → How it works → First offer
- Post-purchase (3 emails): Confirmation → Reaction ask → Re-engage
- Abandoned cart: 1hr → 24hr → 72hr with discount
- Re-engagement: 30/60/90 days after purchase

### KPIs
- Open rate: > 25%
- Click rate: > 3%
- Conversion rate: > 1%

## SEO / Content

### On-Page SEO
- Title tag: Primary keyword + brand
- Meta description: 150–160 chars, include CTA
- H1: One per page, matches primary keyword intent
- Alt text: All images described
- Schema: Product, FAQ, Review markup

### Content Marketing
- Blog posts: Target informational keywords (top of funnel)
- Comparison posts: "X vs Y" capture competitor searchers
- Gift guides: Seasonal, high-intent, linkable
- Frequency: 2–4 posts/month minimum for SEO traction

### Technical SEO
- Core Web Vitals: LCP < 2.5s, FID < 100ms, CLS < 0.1
- Mobile-first indexing: Test on mobile always
- Sitemap: Submit to Google Search Console
- Page speed: Images compressed, lazy loaded

## Influencer / PR

### Tiers
- Nano (1k–10k): Highest trust, often free product only
- Micro (10k–100k): Best ROI, authentic audiences
- Macro (100k–1M): Expensive, lower engagement rate
- Mega (1M+): Brand deals, not relevant at this stage

### Outreach Best Practices
- Personalize every email — reference specific content
- Lead with the free product, not the ask
- Give creative freedom — don't script their post
- Follow up once after 5 days, then move on

### PR Targets — Parenting
BabyCenter, Parents Magazine, Good Housekeeping, Romper, PopSugar Family, The Bump, Fatherly, Scary Mommy, HuffPost Parents, Austin American-Statesman (local), Austin Monthly

### HARO / Qwoted
Monitor daily for: "personalized gifts for kids", "AI for parents", "children's book gifts", "EdTech tools", "gift guides"

## Referral & Retention

### Referral Program
- Structure: Give $5 off, get $5 credit
- Trigger: Post-purchase email #2 (3 days after)
- Placement: Checkout thank you page, account dashboard

### Loyalty
- Discount on 2nd+ books
- Birthday reminders ("Your child's birthday is in 30 days")
- Holiday gift reminders ("Christmas is 6 weeks away")

### UGC Loop
- Ask for reactions in post-purchase email
- Tag prompt on social: "Show us their reaction 😭"
- Reshare best UGC (with permission)
- Build testimonials page on site
