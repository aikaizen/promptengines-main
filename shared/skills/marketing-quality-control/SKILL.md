# Marketing Quality Control — Skill Pair

**Required for all StoryBook Studio and educational marketing work:**

1. **Anti-Slop Skill** — Eliminate hype, generic claims, fabricated social proof
2. **Exceptional Education Skill** — Ensure educational claims meet "best-in-class" standard

---

## Combined Pre-Publish Checklist

### Anti-Slop Filter
- [ ] No "This is not a consultancy" denials
- [ ] No "The old categories dissolve" abstractions
- [ ] No unquantified "rapidly evolving" claims
- [ ] No adjective stacks ("revolutionary, groundbreaking")
- [ ] No hedging preambles ("In today's landscape...")
- [ ] No excessive self-reference ("As an AI...")

### Exceptional Education Filter (if ed-related)
- [ ] Specific curriculum named, not "adaptive learning"
- [ ] Concrete outcomes stated, not "better grades"
- [ ] Parent role preserved, not replaced by AI
- [ ] Refuse slop: named alternatives compared honestly
- [ ] Evidence cited or "research needed" flag raised

### Trust & Honesty Filter
- [ ] No fake testimonials (use real quotes or value statements)
- [ ] Pricing marked as "est." or "from $X" if not finalized
- [ ] Features marked as "planned" or "beta" if not shipped
- [ ] Events marked as "TBD" if not confirmed
- [ ] Every superlative justified with mechanism

---

## Tone Guidelines

### For Parents of Young Children
- **Warm** but not saccharine
- **Clear** but not dumbed-down
- **Honest** about limitations and tradeoffs
- **Respecting** of parent's intelligence and time
- **Concrete** — what exactly happens, when, for how much

### Forbidden Phrases in Parent-Facing Copy
| Don't Use | Why | Instead |
|-----------|-----|---------|
| "Unlock your child's potential" | Meaningless abstraction | "Teach them to read in 20 min/day" |
| "Revolutionary AI-powered" | Hype, no content | Specific capability: "Character stays consistent across pages" |
| "Trusted by thousands of parents" | Fabricated social proof | "We're parents building for our own kids" |
| "Give your child a head start" | Anxiety exploitation | "Start when they're ready, not before" |
| "Don't let your child fall behind" | Fear-based marketing | "Build at their pace" |
| "Seamless integration" | Corporate buzzword | "Works with..." + specific list |

## SEO + Quality

SEO keywords are fine. Keyword stuffing is slop.

**Good**: "Personalized children's books with consistent characters across 34 art styles"

**Slop**: "Best personalized AI-powered storybook creator for kids children toddlers educational learning fun"

---

## Design Alignment

Visual design must match copy tone:
- Clean > cluttered
- Specific > abstract
- Calm > frantic
- Trustworthy > slick

Color psychology for parents:
- Warm oranges (energy, creativity) — ✓
- Clean whites/creams (simplicity, trust) — ✓
- Avoid: aggressive reds (anxiety), cold blues (clinical)

## Exceptional Education + StoryBook Studio Integration

When combining educational content with StoryBook Studio:

**Option 1**: Literacy extension
- Generated stories using FLDOE book themes
- Character comprehension questions
- Vocabulary from Ed Hirsch knowledge domains

**Option 2**: Reading incentive
- Books created after completing 100 Lessons milestones
- "Publish your own" as motivation
- Parent-child co-creation time

**Option 3**: Assessment integration
- Story retelling checks reading comprehension
- Character creation tests descriptive vocabulary
- Sharing with grandparents reinforces pride
