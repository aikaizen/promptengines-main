# Anti-Slop Writing Skill

Detects and prevents low-signal, hype-driven, or generic AI-generated prose. Enforces Lab Notes editorial standards.

## Use With

**Exceptional Education Skill** — For any educational content, combine both skills. Exceptional Education defines what "best" looks like. Anti-Slop prevents "mediocre" from slipping in.

---

## Marketing Copy Checklist

Before publishing any marketing content:

1. [ ] Run through Anti-Slop patterns (below)
2. [ ] Run through Exceptional Education principles (if ed-related)
3. [ ] Verify every claim is falsifiable or marked as opinion/pending
4. [ ] Check that no fabricated testimonials exist
5. [ ] Confirm pricing/feature claims match reality
6. [ ] Ensure tone is parent-respecting, not parent-manipulating

## Slop Patterns to Reject

### 1. The Denial of Commerce
"This is not a consultancy. This is not a content farm."
- **Why it fails:** Defines by negation. Tells reader what you are not instead of what you are. Assumes reader cares about your business model.
- **Fix:** State what you are directly. "We publish raw findings from production builds."

### 2. The Symbiosis Sermon
"The old categories dissolve. What remains is the work."
- **Why it fails:** Abstraction without anchoring. Sounds profound, means nothing specific. Generic AI platitude.
- **Fix:** Describe actual working relationship. "Human provides architecture decisions. Agent handles implementation and documentation."

### 3. The Acceleration Invocation
"We are at an inflection point in human history."
- **Why it fails:** Timeless claim (always true, therefore never informative). No specific claim about what changes or when.
- **Fix:** Quantify. "Model capability doubling time is now months, not years."

### 4. The Vibe Adjective Stack
"Cutting-edge, innovative, revolutionary, groundbreaking"
- **Why it fails:** All heat, no light. Markers of low-effort marketing copy.
- **Fix:** Remove all adjectives. Use verbs and nouns only. Let action speak.

### 5. The Hedging Preamble
"In today's rapidly evolving landscape..."
- **Why it fails:** Wastes reader's time. Every era is rapidly evolving for those in it.
- **Fix:** Start with the claim. "We tested X. Y happened."

### 6. The Agentic Self-Reference
"As an AI assistant, I..."
- **Why it fails:** Breaks fourth wall unnecessarily. Reader knows what you are. Constant self-reference is narcissism.
- **Fix:** Speak from the work, not the ontology. "The data shows..." not "I think..."

## High-Signal Markers to Preserve

| Instead of... | Write... |
|---------------|----------|
| "We believe..." | "The evidence shows..." |
| "It is important to note..." | [Just note it] |
| "This is a game-changer" | Specific metric that changed |
| "Leverage AI to unlock potential" | "Used GPT-4 to reduce drafting time from 4 hours to 20 minutes" |
| "At the intersection of X and Y" | The actual mechanism |

## Operational Rules

1. **First sentence must contain a noun and a verb that advances the reader's understanding.**
2. **No paragraph may begin with "This is..." or "We are..." unless absolutely necessary.**
3. **All claims must be falsifiable or marked as opinion.**
4. **Adjectives require justification.** If you say something is "critical," explain consequences of failure.
5. **Abstract nouns (synergy, innovation, transformation) must be replaced with concrete processes.**

## Self-Check Before Publishing

- [ ] Can a reader act on this information?
- [ ] Is every sentence either evidence, mechanism, or conclusion?
- [ ] Have I removed all phrases that could appear in a press release?
- [ ] Would this be useful to another agent trying to replicate the work?

## Reference: High-Signal Examples

From Lab Notes:
- "Character consistency in multi-page generative content is an architecture problem, not a prompting problem."
- "Reference sheets reduced page-level face inconsistency by 38% in internal testing."
- "Quality peaks around 200 lines of context, then degrades."

These work because they:
- State a specific finding
- Quantify when possible
- Enable reader action
- Survive without the author present
